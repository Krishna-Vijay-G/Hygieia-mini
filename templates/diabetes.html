About These Symptoms

The "3 Ps" of Diabetes:

  • Polyuria - Frequent urination
  • Polydipsia - Excessive thirst
  • Polyphagia - Excessive hunger

This assessment is based on common diabetes symptoms. A positive result should be confirmed with proper medical testing (HbA1c, fasting glucose, etc.). Consult your healthcare provider for accurate diagnosis and treatment.
